Submitted February 6, 2026 (Florida State University, Center for Advanced Power Systems). Presents an energy management strategy for hybrid powertrains (HPT) that adaptively splits power demand between engine and battery pack, explicitly accounting for battery degradation risk. Unlike standard rule-based or optimization-based strategies that ignore aging, this framework integrates battery degradation into the optimization objective, balancing fuel efficiency against long-term battery health. Dual submission to IFAC MECC 2026 and ASME Letters indicates cross-community relevance. Directly relevant to area 4 (Optimal control for electrified powertrains) and area 3 (Safety/dynamics).
