Submitted June 15, 2026. Addresses robust fault detection filter design for continuous-time LTI uncertain systems in open-loop and closed-loop configurations. Handles both parametric and dynamic uncertainties in a unified approach by solving a single Riccati equation; the worst-case disturbance/uncertainty model is obtained via nonlinear optimization and the boundary Nevanlinna-Pick method. Relevant to fault-tolerant control for electrified vehicle systems — sensor faults in EVs (current sensors, wheel speed encoders, battery voltage sensors) degrade state estimation and could be caught with this framework. Related to [[hadji-thomas-2026-arc-adaptive-robust-estimation]] (complementary: robust estimation vs. fault detection). See concept page (future): `fault-detection-control`.
