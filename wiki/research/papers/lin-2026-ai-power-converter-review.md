Submitted June 14, 2026 (Cambridge, Leicester, SJTU, Nanyang). Comprehensive review of AI methods for power-converter-rich electrical systems — encompassing renewable generation, electrified transportation, and inverter-based resources. Organizes literature across converter design, real-time control, system-level operation, and compliance/governance from a life-cycle and deployment-readiness perspective. Addresses strongly nonlinear dynamics, multi-physics design tradeoffs, fast control requirements, and cybersecurity constraints. Relevant to EV powertrain control (motor drives, DC-DC converters, regenerative braking inverters) and provides a taxonomy of AI methods applicable to electrified vehicle power systems. Useful background reference for [[AI-based-vehicle-control]] and [[optimal-control-electrified-powertrain]].
